Review of 2012 and plans for 2013

Igor Anany | 29 Dec 2012

2012 year was very productive for me and my family. I can easily call it the year of changes. We have changed a lot in our life.

Goals achieved in 2012:

  • We have moved to the beautiful New Zealand. That was very exciting process.
  • My wife got pregnant and our kid Nathaniel was born. I was taken part in the birthing process and cut the cord myself.
  • I have got New Zealand approved qualification.
  • I have got good job in New Zealand.
  • We have bought a new car.
  • I have bought Macbook Pro with Retina display.
  • My wife has bought Thunderbolt display.
  • We have learned english enough to communicate with other people and watch movies. Still there are a lot of mistakes but we will work this out.
  • We have launched Easy Words Learning iOS Application and website.
  • We have started yoga classes and tennis.
  • We have traveled to Cape Reinga, Napier, Rotorua, Wellington, Taupo, Ruapehu.
  • We both grown like a professionals.

I believe that 2013 - is the year of stability and success.

Plans for 2013:

  • Launch 2 more iOS applications.
  • Get New Zealand full driving license.
  • Get New Zealand residence visa.
  • Start surfing.
  • Travel to South Island.
  • Start personal blog in english.
  • Move to North Shore.
  • Buy motorbike.
